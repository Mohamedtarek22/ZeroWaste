Who are You?


Zero waste is an Egyptian company registered under the ministry of investment to tackle the growing problem in the waste. We are an environmental and waste management company established to create a sustainable environment by providing consultations, environmental assessments, and audits, waste recycling, advocacy throughout allied acts with partnerships, and collaboration.

Our Services

Recycling

Zero waste delivers set of comprehensive and high-quality recycling services across multidisciplinary context based on our principles and best practices, adaptability to client’s needs and sustainability to all our activities.




.

Commercial and industrial waste collection

We work with businesses of varying sizes and within all sectors across, to help you decide which waste management services, collections and recycling services best fit your needs. From general waste collections, to hazardous waste management, to dry mixed recycling, and much more. No matter what you need from your waste carrier, we are here to help.

Disinfection and cleaning

Zero Waste staff are experts in creating clean, healthy and safe environments. It’s more important than ever to make sure everyone who enters your facility is protected from germs and bacteria that can easily spread from one person to the next. Our commercial cleaning company specializes in disinfection services and will help you reduce the risk of illness in your facility.

We Serve

Wherever we are, whatever we’re doing, we’re deploying best-in-class technologies to make the world a cleaner, greener place to live in. We’re working with all sorts of companies and government bodies to sweep up, scrub, crush, squash and polish so that everything that can be recycled is; and everything that should be spick and span is gleaming night and day.
